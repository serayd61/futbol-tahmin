### Description (4000 char)

```
Futbol Tahmini is a free statistics app providing scientific score predictions for daily matches across European football leagues.

🎯 SCIENTIFIC PREDICTIONS
Every match is analyzed using Poisson distribution with Bayesian shrinkage. Team form, goal averages, and recent performance produce:
• 1X2 probabilities (home - draw - away)
• Both teams to score probability
• Most likely score prediction
• Confidence level (low / medium / high)

⚽ SUPPORTED LEAGUES
• Premier League (England)
• La Liga (Spain)
• Bundesliga (Germany)
• Serie A (Italy)
• Ligue 1 (France)
• Eredivisie (Netherlands)
• Primeira Liga (Portugal)
• Brasileirão (Brazil)
• Süper Lig (Turkey)
• Champions League
• Europa League

🤖 AI MATCH COMMENTARY
Short, informative match commentary powered by Google Gemini. Updated daily.

📊 H2H HISTORY
Recent head-to-head matches, trends and goal averages between any two teams.

🔔 FAVORITE TEAM NOTIFICATIONS
Add your favorite teams and receive push notifications before their matches kick off. No personal info needed.

📈 PREDICTION ACCURACY HISTORY
Transparently see how the model has performed historically — which leagues we predict well, which need improvement. In the Stats tab.

🎨 DARK / LIGHT THEME
Automatic based on your system or manual selection.

🔒 PRIVACY
• No personal information collected (no name, email, location)
• No ads, no tracking, no analytics
• Only push token + favorite team list stored anonymously
• Full privacy policy: serayd61.github.io/futbol-tahmin/privacy.html

⚠️ IMPORTANT
This app provides statistical analysis for educational and informational purposes only. No betting, no gambling, no real-money transactions. Predictions are not guarantees.

📧 FEEDBACK
Questions, bugs, or suggestions: serayd6161@gmail.com
```
